Egypt Travel Guide!

Seasons

Winter

The generally preferred time to visit Egypt is winter, which runs from October to April-May. However, it should be noted that the climate can vary considerably depending on the region..

Egypt's northern coast enjoys a Mediterranean climate, while the central and southern interior regions are characterized by a desert climate. The Red Sea coast also has a desert climate, but it is slightly milder.

The ideal time to visit Egypt would be from November to April, when the mild winter sets in. Temperatures are usually pleasant, rarely falling below 10°C. However, it is important to research the weather conditions specific to the region you plan to visit, as there may be variations and climatic nuances to consider.
