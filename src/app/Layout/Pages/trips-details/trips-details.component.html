Travel Itinerary
Day 1 - Arrival & Introduction to Cairo
Morning
  • Arrival at Cairo International Airport. Upon arrival, you'll be greeted and transferred to your hotel.
  • Check-in & freshen up. Consider staying in a central location like Zamalek or downtown Cairo for easy access to key sights.
Afternoon
  • Lunch at a local restaurant: Experience traditional Egyptian cuisine (like koshari or ful medames) in a well-known Cairo restaurant.
  • Cairo's Tahrir Square: Walk around the iconic square, which is surrounded by historical landmarks like the Egyptian Museum and American University in Cairo. It's a good introduction to Cairo's modern history.
Evening
  • Nile River Cruise: Enjoy a peaceful evening cruise on the Nile River, complete with dinner and live traditional music or entertainment.
