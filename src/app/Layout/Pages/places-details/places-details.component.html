Take a scenic road trip from Cairo to Alexandria with Egypt Knight and uncover the treasures of Egypt’s stunning Mediterranean city. This journey is a perfect blend of history, culture, and breathtaking coastal views. Visit the legendary Great Library of Alexandria, once the world’s most renowned center of knowledge. Stand in awe at Pompey’s Pillar, a towering Roman monument, and explore the Catacombs of Kom El-Shoqafa, an underground necropolis filled with stunning carvings and ancient secrets.
